Exam Alchemy

Transforming Exam Preparation with AI Intelligence

Exam Alchemy is a highly interactive, state-of-the-art educational suite designed to simplify and supercharge exam preparation for teachers and students alike. By combining advanced text parsers with Google's generative AI, we bridge the gap between educational course documents and structured practice assessments.

For Teachers, our dashboard offers full control over multiple question bank uploads, dynamic exam layout customization, and an AI-powered document extraction system. Teachers can drag and drop PDFs or plain text syllabus files, extract core contextual questions in seconds using local or Gemini engines, and save them straight to their persistent Question Banks.

For Students, the platform opens an elegant repository of previous year questions, custom mock papers, and practice assessments—empowering them to practice, analyze progress, and succeed in their academic journeys.
